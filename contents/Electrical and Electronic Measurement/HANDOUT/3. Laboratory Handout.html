Course Objectives

1. Understand the basic principle, construction and application of different types of sensors.

2. To evaluate the behaviour of different types of sensors.

3. To plot the basic characteristics different types of sensors.

4. Designing of Logic gates using sensors.

5. Design of Wheatstone bridge on breadboard.

6. Study of DSO and applications in measurements.

Learning Outcomes (LO)

1. Recognize various materials by employ different sensors.

2. Understand the working of different types of sensors and its applications.

3. Student will be able to control and monitor industrial processes.

4. Analyze various response of the system with the help of Digital Storage Oscilloscope.
