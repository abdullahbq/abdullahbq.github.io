Course Objectives

On the successful completion of the course, students will be able to:

1. Understand the concept of units and dimensions and construction and working of different measuring instruments

2. Develop an understanding of different type of interferences, its causes and methods for its reduction

3. Develop an understanding of construction and working of different AC and DC bridges and its applications

4. Develop an ability to use measuring instruments and AC and DC bridges for measurement

Learning Outcomes (LO)

The Students will be able to:

1. Analyze the performance of industrial measurement systems.

2. Identify different type of transducers used in various real-time processes.

3. Measurement of various electrical quantities.

4. Design the bridge circuits used in measurement task.

5. Operate different waveform analyzers and CRO employed for measurement of electrical qualities.

6. Evaluate the performance characteristic of an instrument.

7. Evaluate the different types of errors in measurements systems.
